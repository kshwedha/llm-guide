Rating Anything (1–10)

A "rating" is just constrained next-token prediction — and it works for anything you can phrase as a 1–10 question. Whether it's a product review, an opinion ("rate Newton's brilliance"), a difficulty estimate, or a divisive food take — we extract the logits over the tokens "1", "2", … "10" at the answer position, softmax them, and either argmax or take the expected value. Same operation, very different distributions.
